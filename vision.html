NUASA Revolution Party Vision

The vision of the Nile University Accounting Students Association Revolution Party is to build a stronger, progressive, accountable, and student-centered NUASA that empowers every accounting student academically, professionally, and socially through purposeful leadership, unity, innovation, and opportunity.

“Together, We Rise.”

Stronger internship partnerships

Creating strong partnerships and bonds with accounting and financial firms to secure better SIWES and internship opportunities for students.

Accessible learning resources

Establishing accessible accounting libraries and academic resource centers to support learning and professional development.

Scholarship & certification support

Securing scholarship opportunities and professional support programs with ICAN and ACCA, especially for students pursuing the FIA stage and other certifications.

Student & staff welfare

Prioritizing the welfare, development, and support of both students and staff within the department.

Accountability and unity

Promoting accountability, transparency, professionalism, unity, and excellence within NUASA.

Financial transparency

Since it’s your money, you deserve to know how it is being spent — we will publish monthly account ledgers with clear, easy-to-read details.

Every voice, every opportunity

Creating a NUASA where every accounting student has a voice, opportunities to grow, and leadership that truly represents them.
